Longer

Anthony is the co-founder and CTO of Terra AI where he leads the development of decision support tools for sustainable natural resource projects related to clean energy and the discovery of critical minerals. He is also a visiting scholar at Stanford University. Anthony received his Ph.D. in Aeronautics and Astronautics from Stanford University in 2021 and remained as a postdoctoral researcher until 2024. His research has focused on the use of algorithmic decision-making for safety-critical applications, emphasizing the creation of robust, reliable autonomous systems. He has developed algorithms for the validation and verification of complex autonomous systems such as autonomous vehicles and aviation subsystems.
